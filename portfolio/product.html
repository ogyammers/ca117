Get thinking

Our product is an app designed to help both the individuals and family’s effect by dementia. The app will include several features such as:

  • Brain testing games from virtual baking to reading and writing tasks.

  • Daily blogging section where it’s users can record either their daily struggles or achievements.

  • Reminders and notification for when and what tablets you should be taking throughout the day.

  • Easy facial recognition into the app to remove the struggle of trying to remember passwords.


The idea behind this app is to exercise and test its user’s memory and try to reduce and in certain cases increases their memory capacity.

Brain Games

One major aspect of this app are the inclusion of in-app games and puzzles. Across the entire app there will be a total of twelve memory testing games, all researched and proven to help the memory of dementia patients. These games are divided into four main categories Household Activities, Games and Puzzles, Brain Training Games and Physical Activities. This leaves its user with a wide range of games to partake in facilitating for every user’s needs.

Blogging Section

Another useful aspect of this app is the addition of the blogging section allowing its users to write daily or even hourly blogs on events throughout their day. These blogs will not only prove useful for their writers as they can look back and see what they had previously completed in the weeks just past, but also their can be an option when creating the app to send these blogs to specific emails. This allows family members who maybe can’t always be there for their friend or family member to connect and feel attached to its user and informs them if they should or shouldn’t be concerned for their wellbeing. Overall, this feature should revolutionise the life of its users for the better and in a recently published survey 88% of people agreed with us.

Reminders and Notifications

This app will include an optional feature that allows its users to set notification of when and what pills they need to take. This information will be set on creating your profile for the app and will be changeable any time in the settings. The app will notify you when you need to take you tablets what tablets you need to take and even an optional photo of the tablet that can be uploaded in the settings. This eliminates all confusion for its user when it comes to taking their pills.

Facial Recognition

The app on opening will include either fingerprint or facial recognition depending on its users model of phone. The purpose of this is to avoid the hassle and stress that comes with trying to remember passwords every time loading into the app.

Our Market Research

Throughout our design and prototype period of this app we conducted endless research on what would be the best features to include into such an app to help dementia patients in their everyday tasks and errands. Our app is a combination and blend of all this research to create an easy to use and navigate app. We used resources such as the internet, books and even newspapers dating back as far as 2013 in the research of this app.
