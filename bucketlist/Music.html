Jazz

Jazz music is a genre that started during the late 19th century. And it is characteristic for its improvisation, syncopated rhythms and compound harmony.

Pop

Pop is the kind of music to lift your spirits up with some strong beats and simple tunes. Brought to you from Electric Pop to K-Pop to Indie Pop and much more!

Alternative

Alternative music is a wide term used to describe any music that does not fit inside mainstream music. It is a umbrella term for multiple genres the most popular being Alt Rock, Alt country and Alt Hip-Hop.

Rap

A musical form of vocal delivery that incorporates "rhyme, rhythmic speech, and street vernacular".

Classical

Classical music is the fundamentals of most modern music, the techniques and music are still used and taught in modern society and has shaped generations.

Rock

Rock is a popular music genre that combines elements of rhythm and blues, jazz, and country music with the addition of electric instruments.
